Evil Herobrine mod for Minecraft PE 1.16.10

Challenge yourself to beat the most terrible creature in the Minecraft world, Herobrine. The evil brother of Notch that died and becomes an evil spirit that frightens up players all around the world. It is an imbalanced rival with such abilities as teleportation, flying, summoning of TNT, spawning of minions. He is teleporting all the time, so it's impossible to hit him, the only way of killing him is to survive to the moment when he starts to blink and unleash all your damage at him.

Credits: TheEnderface, JohnTriztanArtz_MCPEDL



Main features:
  • He is shooting explosive projectiles and using teleportation.
  • Shows up after 1 min and 15 seconds from building a summoning platform.
  • Herobrine curses your world for 8 minutes.




How to install addons:
  1. Download addon into your device
  2. Find and open .mcpack or .mcworld files that you download earlier
  3. Click on it to import into Minecraft Pocket Edition
  4. Open the game
  5. Create new or edit an existing world
  6. Scroll down to Add-ons section and select the ones you want to apply
