Throwable Explosives mod for Minecraft PE 1.2.9

Mod adds new kinds of explosives into the Minecraft PE and allows to throw them. This feature can come in handy when you're fighting with Wither on late stages and want to defeat him quickly. Mod brings four new types of explosives and all of them can be thrown into your foe. Some of them are more powerful than the others, but you will be always at a safe distance, so no need to worry. Explosives will behave differently, for instance, one will be activated on impact and the other will spawn TNT.

Credits: CaptainJoe143






How to install addons:
  1. Download addon into your device
  2. Find and open .mcpack or .mcworld files that you download earlier
  3. Click on it to import into Minecraft Pocket Edition
  4. Open the game
  5. Create new or edit an existing world
  6. Scroll down to Add-ons section and select the ones you want to apply
