Pig Companion mod for Minecraft PE 1.2.0

Mods / 1.2.13
6 052
6.09.17
-6
Mod allows you to gain a new companion - a pig, and not just a regular pig, but its improved version. Tamed pigs can learn supernatural abilities and do crazy stuff, you can create a monster to guard yourself properly. Create an army of pigs with different set of skills and you'll be able to defeat even a Wither. Mod was made for fun, the only disappointment is that you cannot ride them.

Credits: Jimbo_Acob



Main features:
  • You can now tame pigs using carrot.
  • Tamed pigs can be upgraded using one of the following items.
  • Feather - pig will gain fall damage resistance.
  • Pufferfish - applies poison effect on attack.
  • Fish - water breathing "CANT USE WHILE MAGMA CREAM IS ACTIVE!".
  • Magma Cream - fire resistance, but water vulnerability "CANT USE WHILE FISH IS ACTIVE!".
  • Sugar - speed increase.
  • Red Mushroom - makes pig larger.
  • Brown Mushroom - makes pig smaller.
  • Chest - pig will now have a storage.
  • Bow - ability to shoot arrows.
  • Fireball - ability to shoot fireballs.




How to install addons:
  1. Download addon into your device
  2. Find and open .mcpack or .mcworld files that you download earlier
  3. Click on it to import into Minecraft Pocket Edition
  4. Open the game
  5. Create new or edit an existing world
  6. Scroll down to Add-ons section and select the ones you want to apply
