Zombie Apocalypse mod for Minecraft PE 1.1.3

The best mod for those who like survival based games with zombies like DayZ. Mod changes the characteristics of zombies, making them more dangerous and your main enemy. It also gives you a chance to fight back by adding modern weapons and explosives. Invite a few friends and try to survive in this new world, where death awaits you on every corner. This mod provides a true experience of Zombie Apocalypse in Minecraft PE 1.1.3 and can be used in multiplayer battles.

Credits: Hexdro, Twitter



Main features:
  1. Infected Zombies (Zombies, Zombie pigmen, Husks) - won't burn at daylight, moves fast.
  2. Survivors (Villagers) - will turn into zombies if were bitten by Infected.
  3. Bandits (Strays, Wither skeletons, and Skeletons) - will try to kill and rob you, armed with a melee weapon or assault rifle.
  4. Throwing Knife (Snowball) - deals low damage, but has a knockback effect.
  5. Grenade (Egg) - can be thrown and cause an explosion.
  6. Makarov Pistol (Ender Pearl) - deal a decent damage, but has a delay between shots.
  7. AK47 (Fishing Rod) - automatic gun with high amount of damage dealt.
  8. M4A1 (Bow) - makes single and precise shots, needs arrows for shooting.
  9. Green Camouflage (Iron Armor), Leather Armor (Diamond Armor), Plastic Armor (Golden Armor)
  10. Dogs food - used to tame dogs.
  11. Canned food - perfect for surviving.
  12. Villagers are survivors now.






How to install addons:
  1. Download ES File Explorer
  2. Using ES File Explorer go to the Downloads folder on your device
  3. Find .mcpack or .mcworld files that you download earlier
  4. Click it to import into Minecraft Pocket Edition
  5. Open the game
  6. Create a new world or edit an existing world
  7. Scroll down into Add-ons section and select the ones you want to apply
