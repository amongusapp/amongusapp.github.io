Hoverbike - is a new word in traveling across the world in Minecraft PE. Advantages of using this kind of technology allows to fast travel and overcome obstacles of any height. Hoverbike can jump higher than the clouds, up to the very limits of the world. It wont receive fall down damage, but if you need to fix it - use some Iron ingots.

Credits: AlexFirey1411




Hoverbike replaces horse, its silhouette may remind you some kind of a robotic horse.



Sat on hoverbike for a few times until the hearts shows up, then open inventory and put a saddle on it.

How to install addons:
  1. Download addon into your device
  2. Find and open .mcpack or .mcworld files that you download earlier
  3. Click on it to import into Minecraft Pocket Edition
  4. Open the game
  5. Create new or edit an existing world
  6. Scroll down to Add-ons section and select the ones you want to apply
